Karme is a lifestyle brand based in Lisbon which sets out to create a new class of products and experiences. We aspire to create simple, sustainable and healh-oriented products which are in tune with modern living. We distance ourselves from the use of plastics, artificial materials and flavors and commit to organic and sustainable production. Our first line of beverages exhibit those unique properties. They are pro-biotic, low on sugar, yet delicious and refreshing.

Karme Kobucha currently comes in three tasty flavors: Plain, Ginger-Pear and Raspberry.

Over the years we have perfected a unique production and infusion process resulting in a pleasant balance of acidity and infused organic fruits. Please consult our gallery for more insights.

We deliver our beverages in re-usable bottles and whilst they are fully recycable, they seek to extend their lives beyond a single use in your kitchen and household.

If you are interested in offering your customers a healthy non-alcoholic beverage that pairs well with a range of foods, we'd love to hear from you.
